Formulas:
• Revenue: R(Q) = P(Q) × Q where P(Q) is the price at quantity Q and R(Q) is the revenue at quantity Q
• Marginal Revenue: MR(Q) = R(Q+1) − R(Q)
• Marginal Cost: MC(Q) = TC(Q+1) − TC(Q)
• Profit: π = R − TC
